About Ricardo Fenochietto

Ricardo Fenochietto is an Argentine economist, author and international tax policy expert with a distinguished career in public finance.

He has worked extensively with the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and other global organizations, contributing to fiscal policy reforms, tax equity analysis, and VAT system design across Latin America and beyond.

With over 25 years of experience in both academic and policy roles, Fenochietto is known for combining technical rigor with practical insights—especially in the areas of tax administration, public sector economics, and fiscal decentralization.
