👤 About Ricardo Fenochietto

Ricardo Fenochietto is an Argentine economist, author, and international tax policy expert with a distinguished career in public finance. He has worked extensively with the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and other global organizations, contributing to fiscal policy reforms, tax equity analysis, and VAT system design across Latin America and beyond.

With over 25 years of experience in both academic and policy roles, Fenochietto is known for combining technical rigor with practical insights—especially in the areas of tax administration, public sector economics, and fiscal decentralization.

He is the author of several foundational works, including:

  • 📘 Impuesto al valor agregado, a detailed exploration of the economic and legal structure of VAT systems.
  • 📗 Economía del sector público, a comprehensive guide to public sector economics and fiscal impacts.

Fenochietto has published numerous articles and working papers on topics such as tax effort, fiscal federalism, and VAT equity, and has advised governments on improving revenue mobilization and fiscal equity in post-crisis scenarios.

His work bridges theory and implementation, influencing both national policy frameworks and international tax discourse.
